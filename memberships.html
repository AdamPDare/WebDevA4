Memberships and Visiting

Before purchasing a membership, you can visit the club, on our normal, nights. You can only visit up to three times.

The fees on the right, cover all shuttle usage and court fees throughout the season (Sept - April) for all club nights and club tournaments.

When joining late in the season membership fees are adjusted pro rata to reflect the remaining club nights. Please ask for details.

Benefits of a Membership

  • Weekly (Seasonal) club nights - No extra Court or Shuttle Fees
  • Entry to 5 club tournaments
  • Annual membership to Ely Sports Paradise Center
  • Badminton England membership
  • Affiliation to Cambridgeshire Badminton
  • 20% discount at top spin tennis
  • Match/ league play
  • Discounted Coaching
